Common Questions

How do I add another child?
Tap your child's name in the top right, then "Add another child" (Plus subscribers only).

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

Is my data private?
Yes — your child's data stays on your device and is never sold. See our Privacy Policy for details.
